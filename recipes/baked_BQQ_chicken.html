Baked BQQ Chicken Drumsticks

Description

These oven-baked barbeque chicken drumsticks are easy to prep and cook in just 40 minutes. They taste sweet, tangy, and juicy. I used Baby Ray's barbeque sauce for ease and convenience but feel free to use homemade barbeque sauce.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Basic spices and pantry staples make an easy, flavorful marinade.
  • Home cooks appreciate the flexibility to swap oils and sauces without sacrificing the results.
  • Allrecipes member France Cevallos says, "The seasonings and cook time are spot on."

Ingredients & Quantity

  • 1 ½ pounds chicken drumsticks
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 ½ cups barbeque sauce (such as Sweet Baby Ray's), or more to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with foil.
  2. Mix olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder and cayenne together in a large bowl. Add drumsticks and toss to coat. Spread out on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Remove from oven and brush with barbeque sauce; bake for 7 to 8 minutes. Flip drumsticks, brush again with barbeque sauce, and bake an additional 7 to 8 minutes.
  4. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and turn on the oven's broiler.
  5. Brush drumsticks with more barbeque sauce and broil for 3 to 4 minutes. Flip drumsticks, brush with barbeque sauce, and broil until chicken is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, 3 to 4 more minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
